## Project Objectives

- Analyze salary distribution and identify outliers.
- Understand salary growth across experience levels.
- Compare salary variations across company sizes.
- Examine the impact of remote work ratio.
- Perform correlation analysis between key variables.
- Study hiring patterns across different company sizes.

## Insights Summary

### Salary Distribution
- Salaries are right-skewed.
- Most earn between **$80K‚Äì$170K**.
- Median salary is around **$140K**.
- Several high-end outliers above **$250K**.

### Salary vs Experience
- Executive roles earn the highest (~$200K).
- Large jump from Entry (~$80K) to Mid (~$105K).
- Senior-level salaries average around **$155K**.

### Company Size Impact
- Large companies offer the highest median salary.
- Small companies show the widest salary variability.
- Medium companies offer stable, consistent salary ranges.

### Remote Work Ratio
- 51% roles are fully onsite.
- 44% roles are fully remote.
- 5% are hybrid.

### Correlation Analysis
- Experience level has a positive correlation with salary (0.23).
- Remote ratio has a slight negative correlation (‚Äì0.24).
- Currency and location have very weak correlation.

### Hiring Trends
- Medium companies hire the most (especially Senior roles).
- Large companies prefer Mid and Senior-level professionals.
- Small companies hire the least due to limited workforce size.
